About GreenCode

GreenCode is a 10 hour sprint for students who love to build things. We are bringing people together to see what can be made when you stop talking and start coding. Our goal is simple. We want to see what happens when you have 10 hours, a great environment, and no distractions to create something real.

ONE DAY. ONE PROJECT. EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO MAKE IT REAL.
